Minion War

Battle for Bananas

Watch the minions replace the traditional Tanks Game.
Be a part of the excitement!!

How to play the game

Introducing bapple!

Bapple will act as life packs and will boost your energy

Collect bannana, the coins in minion world

Shoot your opponents with shrink guns and watch them shrink!

Shrink brick walls to pass through

But never try to surpass the stone walls!

And remeber we are allergic to water

And the winner is...

Battle on till the time runs out or all others disappear

How to Play?

Rules and Regulations...

Minion with most bananas will win!

Obstacles?

Battle ground contains many obstacles such as,

  • Brick Walls – Players can shoot and break walls. 4 shots will break the wall and it becomes a blank cell.
  • Stone – obstacles. cannot be shot. Bullets cannot pass through.
  • Water – Pits. Cannot go through but can shoot through

Bapples!!

Bapples will add life to minions
